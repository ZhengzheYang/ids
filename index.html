Project Motivation

Review websites play an important role in people’s daily lives. Yelp, a go-to website for restaurants and tourists’ attraction reviews, is always the top choice for online users.

The great impact made by Yelp on people’s daily lives motivates us to select a related dataset for the final project, and for the purpose of this project, we limit the geographical scope to only concerning Pittsburgh city in order to provide more in-depth analysis.
